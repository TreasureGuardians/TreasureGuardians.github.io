History of game

In a distant and magical kingdom, a world full of majestic castles, enchanted forests and mythical creatures. There was a peculiar profession known as "medieval parcel delivery." Where the Client Eduardo, hired some great adventurers for a great mission, to send a very important and urgent package to the mayor of the kingdom Wynthor, since the main road is blocked. It is where our great adventurers will take the most dangerous path to reach the kingdom, The Dark Forest, the lands of the dark lord. They will have to adventure and prepare to fight large and powerful enemies along the way, trying to keep the package safe until they reach their destination. We start the Dandole Video Game in New Game, in the game we will have several functions and actions to do, we will be able to interact with objects, animals and characters. There are different maps, with their own music, atmosphere and interactions. In order to start the mission we will have to talk to Eduardo who is waiting for us inside the house where he will deliver the package to the Mayor. We will begin our journey entering the forests. We will be able to find lost objects on the map, such as armor or weapons. Some enemies will come to us by surprise to attack us, we will have a great animation and a fight event where we can attack or flee. And some enemies where we will have to fight without being able to flee.
